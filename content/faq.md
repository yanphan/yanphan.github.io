**Q: What is the main purpose of this website?**  
**A:** This is a personal space where I document my learning journey, store my research and hands-on projects as a student. Writing things down helps me reinforce my knowledge, and I hope it can also be helpful to those who are on the same path.

**Q: How can I get an update when a new post comes out?**  
**A:** I post about my new post on this Ko-fi [@yanphan](https://ko-fi.com/yanphan) account.

**Q: Where does the knowledge on Yan'Codex come from?**  
**A:** I learn by combining official textbooks, documentation, and reputable sources with modern AI tools that act as my tutors/mentors. That's why sometimes you'll notice the writing has a bit of an "AI flavor"😄.

**Q: What if I see something incorrect in the post?**  
**A:** You can reach me via email: yanphan.dev@gmail.com or click "Suggest Changes" in post to suggest edits. Thank you! Since I'm learning and writing at the same time, mistakes are inevitable. It would be wonderful to receive your feedback and suggestions!
